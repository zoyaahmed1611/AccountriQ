How AccountriQ Works

1. Send Request

Automatically request the required documents from clients.

2. Follow Up

Automated reminders for missing or incomplete submissions.

3. Upload Done

Clients upload files through a guided interface.

4. Extra Security

Files are stored securely with an extra layer of encryption.

5. AI-Based Scanning

Automatically scans and extracts key information from uploaded documents.

6. Data Extraction

Information is saved in structured formats for accounting workflows.

7. Chatbot Assistance

An AI chatbot answers client questions instantly.

8. Client Training

Interactive guidance helps clients submit correct documents with minimal training.
