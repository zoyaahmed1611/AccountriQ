About AccountriQ

AccountriQ is an AI-powered accounting workflow automation tool designed to reduce manual effort, minimize errors, and streamline communication between accountants and their clients. From requesting the right documents to automated follow-ups, AI-based data extraction, and client training, AccountriQ ensures accountants can focus on high-value tasks while clients are guided effortlessly.

Automatic Document Requests

No more chasing clients — requests are automatically generated based on task needs.

Guided Client Instructions

Step-by-step guidance ensures clients submit accurate and complete documents.

AI-Powered Extraction

Scan unstructured files, extract key info, and minimize human errors.

Automated Follow-Ups

Clients are reminded automatically, reducing delays and workload.

Secure & Structured Storage

All data is encrypted, organized, and ready for your accounting software.
